Business Development Executive

Job Description

 Business Overview:
 Techverse, is an offshore software development service based company residing at Ahmedabad (HQ), with extended its infrastructure based at UAE, USA & Australia. With over 25 years of experience, we have employed a team-based approach and a steadfast commitment to continuously evolving our services model and operational foundation, which has resulted in our organic growth. You can visit our company website for further details

Profile Overview:
 As a Lead - Business Analyst, you will be responsible for leading a team of business analysts and playing a critical role in shaping and optimizing our business processes. You will collaborate with cross-functional teams to gather and analyze business requirements, identify opportunities for improvement, and ensure successful project delivery.

Skills:
 Soft Skills, Functional & Technical Analysis, Leadership & Collaboration, Management, Figma, Sketch, Figjam, Draw.io, SharePoint, Google Docs, JIRA, Trello, SQL, GIT.

Key Responsibilities:
 Team Leadership: Lead, mentor, and supervise a team of business analysts, fostering their professional growth and success.
Requirements Gathering: Work closely with stakeholders to elicit, document, and analyze business requirements, user stories, and functional specifications.
Data Analysis: Utilize data analysis techniques to discover patterns, trends, and actionable insights to guide business decisions.
Process Improvement: Identify and recommend opportunities for enhancing business processes, systems, and workflows to increase efficiency and effectiveness.
Project Management: Manage and oversee business analysis activities for projects, ensuring they are delivered on time, within scope, and within budget.
Stakeholder Communication: Act as a liaison between technical teams and business stakeholders, translating business needs into technical requirements.
Quality Assurance: Ensure that solutions align with business requirements and meet quality standards through testing and validation.
Documentation: Create and maintain comprehensive documentation of business processes, requirements, and system configurations.

 Desired Specifications & Qualifications:
 Minimum of 6-7 yrs of proven experience as a Business Analyst, including leadership responsibilities.
Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
Excellent English communication and interpersonal abilities.
Project management experience, including familiarity with project management tools.
Knowledge of industry best practices and standards.
